The Best Movies for Me

My top 3 movies of all time

Citizen Kane (1941)

Considered the greatest film ever made for its revolutionary filmmaking techniques, complex non-linear storytelling, and timeless exploration of power and isolation.

The Godfather (1972)

A masterpiece of storytelling and character development, celebrated for its superb acting, masterful direction, and its poignant and influential portrayal of a criminal family's rise and fall.

2001: A Space Odyssey (1968)

A visionary and technically groundbreaking film that uses stunning visuals and a meditative pace to explore profound themes of human evolution, technology, and our place in the cosmos.
